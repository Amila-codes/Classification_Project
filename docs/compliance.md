## Regulatory Tier

This project is classified as a Limited Risk AI system under the EU AI Act, as it is used for customer churn prediction in a business context.

## Compliance Approach

Although not classified as high-risk, the project considers the following:

- No sensitive attributes in the dataset
- Model decisions are used to support, not replace, human judgment
- Basic evaluation and testing are performed to ensure reliability

## Future Considerations

Future improvements could include fairness evaluation and more transparent model explanations.
