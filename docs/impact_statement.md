**Direct Beneficiaries:**
Customer success teams and marketing departments benefit immediately from better churn predictions. They can identify at-risk customers early and take targeted actions such as personalized offers, support outreach, or service improvements. Customers themselves also benefit by receiving more relevant support and potentially improved service experiences.

**Secondary Ripple:**
Accurate churn prediction can lead to higher customer retention, reducing the need for costly new customer acquisition. This improves business efficiency and long-term profitability. It can also enhance customer satisfaction and loyalty, strengthening brand reputation and reducing resource waste associated with aggressive marketing campaigns.

**Measurable KPI:**
A key performance indicator would be a 15–25% reduction in churn rate within 3–6 months, along with increased customer lifetime value (CLV). Additional metrics could include improved retention campaign success rates and reduced customer acquisition costs.

**Sustainability Link:**
This model supports SDG 12 – Responsible Consumption and Production by encouraging businesses to focus on retaining and better serving existing customers rather than over-consuming resources to acquire new ones. It also indirectly supports sustainable business practices through improved efficiency and reduced waste in marketing and operations.
